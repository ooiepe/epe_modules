What do I do on this page?

The heart of your investigation is the Exploration step. Here students view each of the datasets you have selected and consider the questions you have proposed. Analyzing individual datasets and other pieces of evidence should help students address the challenge you have posed.

  1. First, identify the datasets or other pieces of evidence you would like students to investigate during this activity.
  2. Then, for each dataset add a description and one or more questions to help students analyze each piece.
