What do I do on this page?

The heart of your investigation is the "Exploration" step, in which students view each of the datasets you have selected and consider the questions you have proposed. Analyzing each of the provided datasets, or evidence, should help students answer the challenge question you have posed.

  1. First, identify the datasets or evidence you would like students to investigate during this activity.
  2. Then, for each dataset, add a description and one or more investigation questions.
