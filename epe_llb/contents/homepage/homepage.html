Investigating Ocean Processes with Evidence

Do you want your students to practice interpreting scientific data to draw appropriate conclusions? The Data Investigations available are ideal for introductory undergraduate classes to use as in-class assignments, but they can also be used as engagement or homework activities for upper-level classes. You can also create your own Investigations using the provided step-by-step Data Investigation Builder. The builder will guide you through the process of assembling evidence and questions into an effective inquiry-based investigation.
